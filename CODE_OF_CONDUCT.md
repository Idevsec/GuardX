# **Contributor Code of Conduct**

## **Our Pledge**
We, as contributors and maintainers of the GuardX project, pledge to create an inclusive, welcoming, and safe community. We aim to collaborate respectfully, regardless of experience level, personal identity, or background.

## **Our Standards**
To foster a positive environment, we encourage the following:
- **Be Respectful**: Treat all contributors, users, and maintainers with kindness.
- **Constructive Collaboration**: Criticism should be constructive, focused on the project, and considerate.
- **Inclusivity**: Welcome diverse perspectives and ideas, valuing each participant's unique input.
- **Respectful Communication**: Use welcoming and inclusive language.

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment of any kind (personal, public, or private).
- Disrespectful comments, derogatory remarks, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information (e.g., addresses) without explicit permission.
- Conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## **Our Responsibilities**
Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying and enforcing the standards outlined in this Code of Conduct.
- Addressing unacceptable behavior promptly and fairly.
- Removing or rejecting contributions that do not align with these standards.

## **Scope**
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories and issues.
- Communication channels (e.g., Slack, forums).
- Public and private events related to the project.

## **Reporting Issues**
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers at:  
**[contact.idevsec@gmail.com]**  
All reports will be handled confidentially.

## **Enforcement**
Maintainers will:
1. Investigate reports fairly and confidentially.
2. Respond to issues appropriately, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent exclusions.
3. Clearly communicate their actions and reasoning to the involved parties.
